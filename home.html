Welcome to the Millionaire Game!

"Who Wants to Be a Millionaire" is known for its mix of general knowledge questions,
strategic decision-making, and the potential for life-changing winnings,
making it a popular and enduring game show format worldwide.

Rules and Regulations

  • This Millionaire game consists of 12 questions.
  • Click the "Start Game" button to begin. You will be taken to the game page automatically.
  • Answer questions correctly to progress and earn money. Your earnings will be displayed on the left side of the screen.
  • If you answer a question incorrectly, the game will end, and you'll be directed to another page where you can choose to restart or quit.
  • You can choose to walk away at any time by clicking the walking image on the game page. You will keep the money you have earned so far.
